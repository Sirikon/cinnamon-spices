Thanks for this helpful applet!

The way I use it is that I only use one "pomodoro before long break" - so that my screen gets "blocked" by the overlay. Then I manage the duration of the break myself.

The advantages for me are: My screen really gets my attention to take a break (it is "locked" ;)) and I start the next pomodoro manually and therefore cannot miss its start (as many other users noted too).

So the applet would be perfect for me if it had the option to "block the screen" also for the short breaks, and also start again manually after a short break...

I know that I'm not really a pomodoro specialist, but it helps me to keep up my focus and is a great support for my tired eyes... Thank you very much!
